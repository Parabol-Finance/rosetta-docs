### Deposits

1. Select chain (HyperEVM or Base)
2. Select asset (USDT0 or USDC on HyperEVM; USDC on Base)
3. Transfer assets from main wallet to smart account
4. Router allocates to the optimal vault or market based on current yields

Allocation is immediate. The Router selects the highest-yielding option from the user's enabled set, accounting for margin impact. Minimum deposit is 10 USD equivalent.

Deposits are routed to the highest-yielding option across Morpho and Aave markets on the selected chain.

### Withdrawals

1. Request withdrawal
2. Router exits vault or market position
3. Assets return to main wallet on the same chain

Withdrawals can only go to the user's main wallet. This is enforced at the contract level.
