---
title: Risk & Controls
---

- **Market/execution risk:** Cost‑aware policies; switch thresholds; WL/BL assets.
- **Liquidity risk:** Queue depth monitoring; circuit‑breakers; staged exits.
- **Counterparty/contract risk:** Minimal moving parts; audits; isolated per‑strategy vaults.
- **Operational risk:** Shadow‑mode before enable; kill‑switch; alerts.
