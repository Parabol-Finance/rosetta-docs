Rosetta uses a simple, transparent fee model. Fees apply consistently across all supported chains.

### Routing Fee

Rosetta charges 0.0001% on routed volume.

No fees on:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Holding positions

### Curator Management Fees

Some vault curators charge management fees on yield generated. These fees are deducted at the vault level before APY is reported.

Curator fees are separate from Rosetta's routing fee. The APY displayed in Rosetta already accounts for curator fees — what you see is what you earn.

### Gas Costs

Rosetta sponsors all gas costs. Users pay nothing for:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Rebalancing

The APY displayed is the APY earned.
