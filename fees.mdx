Rosetta uses a simple, transparent fee model.

### Routing Fee

Rosetta charges 0.0001% on routed volume.

No fees on:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Holding positions

### Gas Costs

Rosetta sponsors all gas costs. Users pay nothing for:

- Deposits
- Withdrawals
- Rebalancing

The APY displayed is the APY earned.
