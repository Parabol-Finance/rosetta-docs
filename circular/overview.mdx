## What is Circular

Circular is Rosetta's risk intelligence layer. It provides real-time visibility into lending market risks across Morpho and Aave, transforming on-chain data into actionable risk intelligence. It monitors markets, vaults, and positions continuously.

## Capabilities

Circular exposes 39 tools organized into five categories.

| Category | Tools | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Core Data | 10 | Real-time market, vault, wallet, and protocol metrics |
| Analytics | 9 | Trend analysis, market comparison, yield ranking, position simulation |
| Institutional | 12 | Concentration analysis, stress testing, whale intelligence, compliance |
| Research | 3 | Composite advisors combining multiple analyses into reports |
| Credit Ratings | 5 | AAA-D credit ratings for markets and vaults |

## How It Works

Circular collects data from lending protocols (Morpho Blue, Aave V3), normalizes it into a unified data model, applies risk models and scoring, and surfaces insights through multiple interfaces.

All data is derived from on-chain state. Risk scores, credit ratings, and alerts are computed deterministically from observable protocol data.
